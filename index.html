About the Game

It's basically a turf war, but with rainbow trails

The whole point is that your pet can barely walk straight. Players try to complete simple objectives, but physics makes everything harder — and funnier. You never know what's gonna happen - that's half the fun.

How To Play

Move, spread, dominate

  • Control Your Character

    Navigate smoothly across the arena.

    Trails

    Your movement paints the ground.

    Opponents

    the Round

Features

Looks like a paint bomb went off, but the rules? Dead simple.

Color Crash Arena delivers fast arcade gameplay where quick and dynamic matches keep you on your toes. The core territory mechanics ensure that space control is key to your victory.

With a deep color-based strategy, you always have absolute visual clarity of your progress across the board The game features accessible controls making it easy to start instantly, while the intense action guarantees short sessions that will have you coming back for just one more round.
